The Signal Bedrooms

Within the bedrooms, Puzzle created the beds, shelving and cabinetry, maximising storage space within each room. Under-bed storage with leather handle details reference the hanging headboards, accentuating the contemporary industrial aesthetic, whilst floating bookshelves add playfulness to each space.

  • White-washed Ash cabinet doors, panels & shelves with recessed Bronze handle details.
  • Leather loops and handles and integrated upholstered headboards.

The Etch Bedroom

Within the master bedroom, the contemporary wardrobes make a statement, elevating the look and feel of the space. The dark navy spraywork is elegantly complemented by raw Brass and Chestnut leather handles, whilst the Stone Oak cabinet internals and drawers offer a tasteful contrast. Integrated door sensors ensure that the contents of the wardrobes are illuminated when opened, utilising LED hanging rails.

  • Spraywork in Little Greene Basalt 221.
  • Brass and Chestnut leather handles and stone Oak internals.

The Decca Bedroom

In the bedroom, light-grey stained Oak Panels create a tranquil ambiance and offer a subtle contrast to the white-washed Oak used throughout the living spaces, whilst stained Oak flooring with solid nosing completes the contemporary aesthetic. The wardrobe is hidden behind custom Oak doors that sit flush with the wall panelling.

  • Light-Grey stained crown cut Oak veneered panels.
  • Stained engineered Oak Flooring.
